## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.8+ (First Class is expected)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** Excellent CS, mathematics, or quantitative background with research accomplishments.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you hold a degree from an institution where English is the sole language of instruction.
* **GRE Requirements:** Not Accepted (will not be read)
* **Application Fee:** $105
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Easily requested on the online application. Harvard SEAS routinely approves fee waivers for qualified international candidates with financial need.
