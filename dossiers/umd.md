# PhD Admissions Dossier: University of Maryland

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #140
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #9
* **Target Department:** Department of Computer Science / College of Information (INFO)
* **Research Reputation:** Leading CS school near Washington D.C., with strong ties to federal health agencies (NIH, FDA) and public policy hubs.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Center for Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, Human-Computer Interaction Lab (HCIL)
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $34,000 - $38,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $2,833 - $3,166
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (State of Maryland employee health benefits)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Graduate Assistantships (GA), Graduate Research Assistantships (GRA), and Graduate Teaching Assistantships (GTA) cover tuition.
* **Other Support:** State/department travel funds for conference presentations.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.3+ minimum (First Class is prioritized)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, Information Science, Math, or Engineering.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you completed a degree from a country where English is the official language. Nigeria is officially on UMD's English-speaking waiver list.
* **GRE Requirements:** Optional / Not Required
* **Application Fee:** $75
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available through the Graduate School portal under financial need guidelines.

## 6. Step-by-Step Admission Strategy
1. **Identify Projects:** Read 2-3 recent papers by Philip Resnik and Philip Resnik. Note down how their methodologies align with your past medical database knowledge discovery or social media parsing.
2. **Draft Supervisor Email:** Reach out in October. Keep it under 250 words, attach your CV, cite their recent paper, and highlight your MPhil and first-class status.
3. **Secure Reference Letters:** Ask academic advisors who can vouch for your programming and research capabilities.
4. **Draft Statement of Purpose (SOP):** Structure it logically: 25% past background/MPhil research, 25% your publication achievements, 25% why University of Maryland is the ideal fit, and 25% your future research vision under the matched faculty.
5. **Secure Fee Waiver:** Request a fee waiver via the graduate school portal as early as November.
