# PhD Admissions Dossier: Princeton University

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #22
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #14
* **Target Department:** Department of Computer Science
* **Research Reputation:** World-class, theoretical and applied CS powerhouse, offering exceptional funding and academic freedom.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Center for Information Technology Policy (CITP), Princeton Genomics Institute, Princeton NLP Group
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $51,516 - $54,216
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $4,293 - $4,518
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (Student Health Plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Research Assistantships (RA) and Teaching Assistantships (TA) are standard starting in the second year. First year is typically fellowship-funded.
* **Other Support:** Generous conference travel funding, computer purchase stipend, and emergency funds.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ preferred (First Class is highly competitive)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** Strong computer science, mathematics, or related quantitative background.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Automatically waived if English is the primary language of instruction of the institution (Nigeria qualifies with a Medium of Instruction letter).
* **GRE Requirements:** Not Accepted (will not be reviewed if submitted)
* **Application Fee:** $75
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available via the Graduate School for financial hardship and diversity programs. Very supportive of sub-Saharan African applicants.
