# PhD Admissions Dossier: University of California, Berkeley

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #12
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #4
* **Target Department:** Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (EECS)
* **Research Reputation:** Leading public institution globally with strong commitments to public service, healthcare AI, and algorithmic justice.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Berkeley AI Research (BAIR), Center for Information Technology Research in the Interest of Society (CITRIS)
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered (includes out-of-state tuition coverage)
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $46,000 - $50,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,833 - $4,166
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (SHIP)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Combination of Graduate Student Researcher (GSR) and Graduate Student Instructor (GSI) positions.
* **Other Support:** Travel grants, unionized graduate benefits, student wellness subsidies.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.7+ (First Class required)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, Computer Engineering, or Mathematics with strong research capacity.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you completed a basic degree from a recognized institution where the language of instruction is English.
* **GRE Requirements:** Not Accepted (will not be read)
* **Application Fee:** $155
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Highly competitive for international students; must apply directly to the Graduate Division. Recommend contacting the department early.
