# PhD Admissions Dossier: University of Washington

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #76
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #7
* **Target Department:** Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering
* **Research Reputation:** Leading CS school in the Pacific Northwest, renowned for health informatics, computational biology, and HCI.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Allen School Health and Wellness Group, Center for Biomedical Informatics
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $42,000 - $45,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,500 - $3,750
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (Graduate Appointee Insurance Plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Research Assistantships (RA) and Teaching Assistantships (TA) are standard, covered by a strong graduate union contract.
* **Other Support:** Travel grants, high-performance computing resources, and direct industry collaboration with Microsoft/Amazon/Google.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ (First Class is competitive)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, Computer Engineering, or Data Science with strong programming skills.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Exempt if degree is from an English-medium institution (Nigeria qualifies). Must submit transcript and proof of English instruction.
* **GRE Requirements:** Optional (not heavily weighted)
* **Application Fee:** $85
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available for international students via the Allen School application portal under documented financial hardship.

## 6. Step-by-Step Admission Strategy
1. **Identify Projects:** Read 2-3 recent papers by Su-In Lee and Su-In Lee. Note down how their methodologies align with your past medical database knowledge discovery or social media parsing.
2. **Draft Supervisor Email:** Reach out in October. Keep it under 250 words, attach your CV, cite their recent paper, and highlight your MPhil and first-class status.
3. **Secure Reference Letters:** Ask academic advisors who can vouch for your programming and research capabilities.
4. **Draft Statement of Purpose (SOP):** Structure it logically: 25% past background/MPhil research, 25% your publication achievements, 25% why University of Washington is the ideal fit, and 25% your future research vision under the matched faculty.
5. **Secure Fee Waiver:** Request a fee waiver via the graduate school portal as early as November.
