# PhD Admissions Dossier: Columbia University

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #34
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #16
* **Target Department:** Department of Computer Science / Department of Biomedical Informatics
* **Research Reputation:** Ivy League giant in NYC with leading programs in clinical NLP, AI policy, and database systems.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Data Science Institute (DSI), Biomedical Informatics (DBMI) Lab, Columbia NLP Group
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $48,000 - $52,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $4,000 - $4,333
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (Aetna Student Health Plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Fellowship in Year 1; combination of Graduate Research Assistantships (GRA) and Teaching Assistantships (TA) in Years 2-5.
* **Other Support:** Conference travel grants up to $1,500/year; relocation support occasionally available.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ (First Class required)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, Data Science, Math, or Engineering with strong programming foundations.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Exempt if degree is from an institution where English is the sole language of instruction (requires Medium of Instruction letter).
* **GRE Requirements:** Optional / Not Required for CS PhD
* **Application Fee:** $115
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available via the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS) for financial need. Must apply early.
