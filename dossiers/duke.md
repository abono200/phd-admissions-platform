# PhD Admissions Dossier: Duke University

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #100
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #25
* **Target Department:** Department of Computer Science / Department of Biostatistics & Bioinformatics
* **Research Reputation:** Highly prestigious private research institution with massive medical school and biomedical informatics collaborations.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Duke Center for Research in Public Health, Duke ML Group
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $42,000 - $45,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,500 - $3,750
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (Student Blue health insurance)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Combination of fellowships (1-2 years) and graduate research assistantships under faculty advisors.
* **Other Support:** Conference travel funding ($1,000/year); computing equipment provided.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ (First Class required)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, mathematics, statistics, or biological sciences with quantitative focus.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you earned a degree from an English-medium institution (Nigeria qualifies).
* **GRE Requirements:** Optional / Not Required
* **Application Fee:** $95
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available through the Graduate School for diversity, financial hardship, and selected international organizations.
