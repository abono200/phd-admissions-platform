# PhD Admissions Dossier: Carnegie Mellon University

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #58
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #1
* **Target Department:** School of Computer Science (SCS)
* **Research Reputation:** Ranked #1 for Computer Science. Composed of specialized departments (MLD, LTI, HCII, CSD) with massive healthcare and social systems research.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Machine Learning Department, Language Technologies Institute, Auton Lab
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $45,000 - $48,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,750 - $4,000
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (University Student Health Plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Most students are funded via Research Assistantships under their advisor. TA positions are required for 1-2 semesters.
* **Other Support:** Conference travel support, state-of-the-art computational infrastructure (clusters).
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.6+ preferred (First Class is competitive)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** Strong computer science, algorithmics, and mathematics background.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you obtained a degree from an English-speaking country. For Nigeria, you can request a waiver by submitting a Medium of Instruction letter to the department admissions chair.
* **GRE Requirements:** Optional or Not Accepted (varies by SCS department; Machine Learning Dept does not accept GRE)
* **Application Fee:** $125
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available for international students via the SCS Graduate Admissions website during the application period.

## 6. Step-by-Step Admission Strategy
1. **Identify Projects:** Read 2-3 recent papers by Zachary Lipton and Zachary Lipton. Note down how their methodologies align with your past medical database knowledge discovery or social media parsing.
2. **Draft Supervisor Email:** Reach out in October. Keep it under 250 words, attach your CV, cite their recent paper, and highlight your MPhil and first-class status.
