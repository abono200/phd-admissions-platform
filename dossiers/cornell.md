# PhD Admissions Dossier: Cornell University

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #16
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #11
* **Target Department:** Department of Computer Science / Department of Information Science
* **Research Reputation:** Ivy League leader in the intersection of computing, society, and policy with campuses in Ithaca and NYC (Cornell Tech).
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Cornell Information Science, Center for Data Science, Cornell Tech Health Tech Hub
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $45,000 - $48,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,750 - $4,000
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (Student Health Plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** All PhD students receive 12-month support via Fellowships, RAs, or TAs.
* **Other Support:** Conference travel grants from the Graduate School; equipment support (laptops) is standard.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ (First Class is highly competitive)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** CS, Information Science, or related quantitative field with strong mathematical foundation.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Exempt if degree is from an institution where English is the sole language of instruction (Nigeria qualifies).
* **GRE Requirements:** Not Accepted (will not be read)
* **Application Fee:** $105
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Available for international applicants. You must request the waiver in the online application portal based on financial need.

1. **Identify Projects:** Read 2-3 recent papers by Emma Pierson and Emma Pierson. Note down how their methodologies align with your past medical database knowledge discovery or social media parsing.
