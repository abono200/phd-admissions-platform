# PhD Admissions Dossier: University of Michigan

## 1. University Overview
* **QS World University Ranking (2026/2027):** #33
* **CSrankings.org Ranking:** #12
* **Target Department:** Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) / School of Information (UMSI)
* **Research Reputation:** Premier public research university. The School of Information (UMSI) is world-renowned for health informatics, and CSE leads in medical ML.
* **Key Research Laboratories:** Michigan AI Lab, Center for Healthcare Engineering and Patient Safety (CHEPS)
* **Core Strengths:** Health Informatics, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Computational Policy.

---

## 2. Funding Analysis
* **Funding Guaranteed:** Yes, 100% guaranteed for 5 years
* **Tuition Waiver:** 100% covered
* **Annual Stipend Range:** $42,000 - $46,000
* **Monthly Stipend Range:** $3,500 - $3,833
* **Health Insurance Coverage:** 100% covered (GradCare plan)
* **Assistantship Dynamics:** Typically funded via Graduate Student Research Assistantships (GSRA) or Graduate Student Instructorships (GSI). Graduate union is very active.
* **Other Support:** Rackham Graduate School travel grants; high-end computing resources.
* **Summer Funding:** Typically covered via summer Research Assistantships (included in the annual range above).
* **Funding Duration:** 5 Years (guaranteed subject to satisfactory academic progress).
* **Renewal Requirements:** Maintain a minimum GPA (usually 3.0), complete required teaching assignments (usually 1-2 semesters), and show continuous progress on thesis research.

---

## 3. Admissions Requirements
* **Minimum GPA Expectations:** 3.5+ (First Class is highly competitive)
* **Preferred Academic Background:** Computer Science, Informatics, Math, or Engineering.
* **Master's Degree Required:** No (Direct admission from BSc is possible, but your MPhil is a massive advantage).
* **Programming Skills Expected:** Python, C/C++, R, or similar language. Ability to build ML/DL pipelines (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and manage databases (SQL, Pandas).
* **Mathematics Background:** Strong linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
* **Research Experience:** Highly preferred. Evidence of independent research (such as a thesis) is critical.
* **Publication Expectations:** Highly valued. At least one co-authored paper in a peer-reviewed venue or workshop is preferred.
* **English Language Requirements & Waiver:** Waived if you earned a degree from an institution where English is the sole language of instruction. Nigeria is officially recognized by the Rackham Graduate School for waivers.
* **GRE Requirements:** Not Accepted (will not be read or used)
* **Application Fee:** $90
* **Fee Waiver Availability:** Automatically available for applicants from selected countries (including Nigeria in some departments), or via Rackham Graduate School waiver forms.
